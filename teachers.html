OUR TEACHERS

Know our best educators

Our classroom is an intellectual hour filled with fun and frolics, grasps and grips, smiles and praises, warm hugs and lasting memories; this is possible only cause of our Teachers!

Teachers at respond right go through intense training program to understand the concept of Right brain education (RBE) and impart the same through lot of intellectual fun loving activities in our class. Our Teachers contribute immensely towards development of young minds. They not only work on the correct methodologies but also keep the class active and involved by their positive vibes. Thus by implying the flow of knowledge they are the torch bearers of Gen-Next.

Rutu Karnani


Curriculum Head

A vibrant, multidimensional and a devoted mom. She is a Psychology Graduate with a Diploma in Commercial Arts and Animation.

Her experience varies from being an Artist, Graphic Designer, novice in Textile industry and now an Educationist. Birth of her two children made her realize the different ways children learn and the need to support their growth process.

She loves engaging children in the relevant content. That’s where she found her mantle in Respond Right.

Shivangini Rathore


Head Teacher

An enthusiastic Top - grade teacher !

With scholarship in theatre and MBA degree she has rich experience in various fields, she has been president in geography and dramatics at school, worked in a multinational bank and even experienced her life as a RJ with RED FM. Her passion and love for educating children brings her to respond right education.

With her positive energy and charisma she makes learning interesting, which children absorb and store as lasting memories.


Shailee Sheth


Senior Teacher Trainer, Curriculum Developer

A graduate in psychology, with a Diploma in Early Childhood Education, Shailee is driven and passionate about teaching children.

She has a 7 year experience in the field of education. Her expertise varies from teaching and training in Speech and Drama, Creative Writing and Right Brain Education.

Additionally, she is a creative content writer and has a diploma in creative writing. With an experience in developing content for various educational institutions and websites, Shailee brings a fresh creative wave to RESPOND RIGHT EDUCATION.
