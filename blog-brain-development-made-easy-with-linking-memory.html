Brain Development made easy with Linking Memory

It is a known fact that the brain development in a child starts taking place from a tender age. The kind of conditioning given to the child contributes to the enhancement which is carried forward into adulthood. I have seen so many parents drooling over their kids to get performance based results rather than getting them inclined towards understanding and learning of the subject matter. Just cramming of the information makes them susceptible to shortening of the memory (gradual degradation of the hippocampus) and disrupts their way thinking. It is the peak time when these loopholes need to be taken into light and necessary measures are taken to correct them.

One saving grace for our future generation is the right brain education. Yes you heard it right! Studies have found that the activity of linking memory with the help of unlinked story enables a child to think out of the box. This helps the child to subsequently develop a correlation between unidentified objects and develop a suitable framework or a story which is perceived by them. An active memory is one of the most cognitive abilities in children. With the help of linking the various thoughts and contracting them into one suitable story involves the production of many thoughts. This gives the children a competitive edge by venturing into the new conventional styles of learning. They are able to understand things from different perspectives, read, remember and put them into a connection from the different ideas generated into their minds.

I being a teacher understand the cognitive strengths and weaknesses in a child. However, when it comes to identifying the areas of learning it is wise to push them into a series of tasks like linking memory and let them develop into their own skin. The scores of one child be higher than the other however, a detailed assessment is required to identify the strengths and weaknesses. What matters is that we make the journey memorable for them than the destination. For example, there is technically no connection between two objects like cloud and information. However, the valid connection made between the two is due to the out of the box thinking through linking memory technique which has made them billionaires today!

I hereby compel all the teachers out there to make the classrooms more learning friendly rather than a platform for information cramming. Memory linking skills are a very crucial part of their personal development. These things contribute and have an impact on the child’s leaning abilities. We all need to develop them into fine individuals who are self motivated on an academic as well as personal front. These activities contribute to so many benefits such as:

The child becomes more explorative, learning with play becomes fun.

Is self motivated and gains knowledge without stuffing of information.

These activities make them capable of handling the life’s challenges.

Give a realistic meaning to unrealistic things.

Path breaking tendencies developed at a young age.

More learning oriented than result based.


So what are you guys waiting for? Catch hold of the nearest right brain education centre and make your child bloom.
